Static Visualizations

Shot Distribution Across the Court

This visualization highlights the distribution of shot attempts across different zones on the court. It reveals the Celtics' emphasis on three-point shooting, with a high concentration of attempts from beyond the arc, reflecting their modern offensive strategy.

Scoring Efficiency by Shot Type

This graph showcases scoring efficiency based on various shot types. It emphasizes the Celtics' success in catch-and-shoot situations, which stems from their effective ball movement and ability to create open looks for their players.

Shot Frequency and Success Rates

This visualization compares shot frequency and success rates between different areas on the court. The Celtics have shifted their focus to high-efficiency areas, such as the paint and three-point line, while reducing reliance on mid-range attempts.
