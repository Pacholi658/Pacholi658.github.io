"The Green Machine: Using statistic visualizations to better understand the Celtics' current success in the NBA."

Project Introduction

As students in Boston, we feel as though the history the Boston Celtics have accomplished this past season warranted a reason to study the effectiveness of shot locations on a court and how it can correlate to a team's success. With the Celtics' storied legacy and recent performance, understanding the nuances of basketball strategy, including zone range and action types, has become even more relevant. It is interesting to research how the different variables in basketball affect the game itself.

By analyzing these shot patterns and the contexts in which they are taken, we hope to uncover insights into how modern teams optimize their offense and whether certain trends can be linked to a consistent winning record.
